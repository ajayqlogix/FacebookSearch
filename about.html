FAQ:

  • What is FacebookSearch?

    FacebookSearch is a frontend for the public search service that Facebook released on April 21st, 2010. All it does is draw attention to the information that Facebook now makes publicly available about its users.

    Our goal is to get Facebook to restore the privacy of this information, so that FacebookSearch no longer works.

  • What has Facebook done wrong?

    Facebook has made two clear mistakes here. First, they do not do a good job of indicating how public each piece of information you share on the site will be.

    Second, they change the rules far too often. If you understood Facebook's privacy settings two years ago (or even six months ago) that information would be worse than useless with today's bewildering settings.

  • Who are we?

    We are two Facebook users concerned about privacy. We work as software engineers in the San Francisco Bay Area

  • What should I do with my Facebook account?

    You could delete it or fix your privacy settings.
